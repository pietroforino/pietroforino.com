I'm an Italian multidisciplinary designer born in Milan and specilized in digital and interaction design. In the last period, I have been specializing in the relationship between human beings and technology, analyzing and speculating on how the landscape of interaction design and our society is changing and will change with the advent of Artificial Intelligence.

My experimental side led me to understand technology as a means of shaping ideas, making it the centre of mine design concept. My meaning of design finds translation in the interactive installation, where the focus is on the high capacity to move a reflection in the users. I focus on exploring human emotions and the relationship between society and technology through speculations, experiences and reflections on the self and others.

Furthermore, my works have been exhibited at venues such as the Salone del Mobile in Milan and Designforum in Graz, and I collected a couple of press and international awards.
